Capstone Project

Audio-Assisted Object Identification and Geolocation Navigation for the Blind

  A wearable device that helps blind people identify necessary objects within their homes to give them a fair chance to live independently. It allows them to identify the registered faces of their family members. With the integration of Google Assistant, the blind can navigate on the road with the help of object identification and obstruction detection in front of them. It includes a mobile app that will help their family members track the location of the blind person.
